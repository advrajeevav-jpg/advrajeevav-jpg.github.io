Your Rights as a Consumer

As per the Consumer Protection Act, 2019, every Indian consumer has the right to safety, fair treatment, accurate information, and legal remedies when wronged by a product or service.

1. Key Consumer Rights

  • Right to Safety: Protection against hazardous goods and services.
  • Right to Information: Details about quality, quantity, price, and origin must be clearly disclosed.
  • Right to Choice: No forced selling; you have the freedom to choose.
  • Right to be Heard: Grievances must be acknowledged and resolved promptly.
  • Right to Redressal: You can claim compensation for unfair trade or defective goods/services.
  • Right to Consumer Education: Awareness of your rights is protected under law.
