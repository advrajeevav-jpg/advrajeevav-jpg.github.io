Professional Journey

Advocate A V Rajeev began his legal practice in 1993, enrolling with the Bar Council of Kerala. Over the past three decades, he has established himself as a prominent figure in the civil litigation landscape of Kozhikode (Calicut).

Known for his calm demeanor and meticulous preparation, Adv. Rajeev believes in a client-first approach. He understands that for many, stepping into a courtroom is an intimidating experience. His practice is built on the philosophy of transparency—ensuring clients understand every step of the legal process.

Areas of Specialization

While his practice spans various branches of civil law, he holds specific expertise in:

  • Motor Accident Claims (MACT): Securing fair compensation for victims of road accidents.
  • Insurance Law: Fighting against unjust rejection of life and medical insurance claims.
  • Land & Property Disputes: Handling complex title suits, partition suits, and boundary disputes.
